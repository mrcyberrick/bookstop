About the Shop

One of a Kind.

— ✦ —

"Take a few minutes from your day and stop in to see us — a one of a kind store right here in Rockaway Borough." — Ray

Ray & Judy opened their doors in 1984 with a simple idea: great books at prices that make sense. Four decades later, the shop on West Main Street is a beloved local landmark — stacked floor to ceiling with new and slightly-read books, fresh comics every Wednesday, trading cards, vintage maps, children's books, and surprises around every corner.

What We Carry

— ✦ —
01

Books

New & slightly-read paperbacks at half cover price. Dover Thrift Editions from $1–$5. Organized by subject & author.

02

Comic Books

New shipment every Wednesday. Casual readers to serious collectors. Bags, boards, boxes & albums in stock.

03

Trading Cards

Sports & gaming cards by the pack or box. Pokémon, Magic: The Gathering, baseball & more.

04

Maps

Hagstrom, Rand McNally, USGS Topo. Plus the rare 1868 & 1887 Morris County Atlas reprints — exclusive here.

05

Children's Corner

A full quarter of the store devoted to kids — books, reading guides & toys for younger readers.

06

Collectibles

Weird NJ issues #1–38, records, vintage magazines, local history & NJ-customized greeting cards.

Copy & fax services also available. Can't find something? Ask Ray — he knows where everything is.
